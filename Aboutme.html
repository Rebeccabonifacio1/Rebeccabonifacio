About Me

I am a current undergraduate in my third year at James Madison University. I'm a double major studying Media Arts and Design and Communication Studies . Within my programs of study, I have concentrations in Journalism, Advocacy Studies and Pre-Law, as well as a minor in Political Communication. My experience at James Madison University has been highlighted by my outside involvements. Throughout my time at JMU, I have had the honor of serving my university through being a Student Ambassador , an organization that offers volunteer campus tours to prospective families and students. This organization has helped me grow into the best version of myself. I have become someone who can recognize the good and bad within their involvements and work to fix the things that they don't agree with. I have served in many leadership roles in this organization, but most memorably, I worked on the 2019 Executive Board as Historian. This position focused on our organization's public image, and I was responsible for providing videography and photography services, making merchandise, and running our organization's social media .

Student Ambassadors 2019

My involvements outside of Student Ambassadors vary tremendously, from being in Phi Alpha Delta, a pre-law, professional fraternity, to being a member of EDM Club, an organization that focuses solely on love and appreciation for Electronic Dance Music. I even have a radio show called Radio 51 on JMU's local college station, WXJM, where my roommate and I discuss different conspiracies! Overall, I am somebody who works hard to find the aspects of life that might go unnoticed and revel in originality. I am a creative who is constantly looking for new ways to express this urge. I love travel, spring, the smell of the earth after it rains, and anything nostalgic. At my very core, I'm still figuring it all out and have difficulty describing who I am and who I want to become, but it's all part of the journey.
